## What Talent Edge is today

Talent Edge is a working platform you can open in a browser right now — not a concept deck or a prototype that falls over.
Live demo: **https://talent-edge-ten.vercel.app**

There are two entry points: a **Graduate** path (the candidate's assessment experience) and an **Admin** path (the recruiter dashboard, candidate pipeline, profiles, and reporting).

The platform already covers full psychometric assessment across five potential dimensions, pipeline management with bulk shortlisting, individual candidate profiles with AI-generated screening summaries and auto-generated reports, self-service accessibility accommodations, a scheduling flow, and a self-service assessment configuration screen.

## What we've already captured from you

We have this on record. Don't re-cover it unless you're correcting something.

**Pain points (top 5):**
- Applicant screening is still largely manual — even with an ATS, generating an initial shortlist takes at least a full day
- Disability and neurodiversity management is manual — adjustments handled by email and phone, no self-service
- System setup and configuration spans multiple systems, each requiring separate configuration and re-testing before each intake
- Applicant engagement and experience — program managers field large volumes of candidate questions; feedback reports need to be auto-generated
- Scheduling — self-booking has improved but reschedule and cancel workflows remain predominantly manual

**Amberjack gaps:**
- Assessment inflexibility — everything bespoke costs money; no middle ground between logo-only and fully custom
- Not self-service — clients cannot configure assessments, update branding, or adjust scoring without going through the Amberjack team
- Bespoke costs are prohibitive for small and medium-sized programs

**ATS landscape (15 platforms documented):**
- Top 5 by market prevalence: PageUp, Springboard (govt), Workday, Success Factors, Oracle HCM
- Others: Push Apply, Eightfold, LiveHire, SmartRecruiters, Taleo, Gradsift, Jobadder, Greenhouse, Acendre, Elmo

**Lifecycle model:**
- Attract → Assess → Select → Offer → Keep Warm → Onboard → Develop → Track Talent
- Recruitment = first four stages. Development = last four. Most tools handle one half. Talent Edge connects them.

**Grad-Engage:**
- Focused only on the Keep Warm phase; gets traction because nothing else addresses it well
- We see this as an opportunity — Keep Warm is a first-class lifecycle stage in Talent Edge

**Assessment philosophy:**
- Assess for potential, not privilege — a potential-based process levels the playing field for candidates who haven't had access to prestigious internships, travel, or top-tier education
- Our 5-dimension model: Adaptability, Cognitive Agility, Emotional Intelligence, Collaboration, Drive
- Amberjack's model for comparison: Digital Mindset, Applied Intellect, Creative Force, Grit
